About Our Bakery

Founded in 2025, we are a startup from Columbia, MO in the USA. Originally, each recipe came from family and family friends and have spread to the local shop! In the future, improvements to:

  • Seasonal and specialty menu items
  • Recipe books and design patterns
  • More about the recipe creators
  • Interior decoration and rennovation
  • Platform on social medias and advertisement

Will be made. Keep a look out for future plans, show support through social media! Ask for allergy concerns, although a list of allergens will be listed online as well.

Lastly, enjoy your stay at SweetsBerry and report any feedback through the Contact tab!
